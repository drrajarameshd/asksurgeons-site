Welcome to AskSurgeons.com

AskSurgeons.com is your trusted platform for getting reliable second opinions from expert surgeons in Hyderabad. We understand that when it comes to surgery, patients and families often feel anxious, unsure, and in need of clear guidance.

Our mission is simple: to bring trusted surgical expertise closer to you, so you can make confident healthcare decisions.

Who We Are

AskSurgeons.com was founded by a group of passionate doctors with one common goal — to make specialized surgical advice accessible to everyone.

  • Dr. Raja Ramesh, MD (Osmania) – Consultant Physician, focused on holistic patient care and personalized medical guidance.
  • Dr. Karthik, MS, MCh (PGI Chandigarh) – Renal & Pancreatic Transplant Surgeon with deep expertise in advanced surgical care.

Together, we’ve built a network of highly qualified surgeons across all specialties — including General Surgery, Orthopedics, Neurosurgery, Cardiac Surgery, Urology, Oncology, Gastroenterology, and more.

What We Do

  • Provide second opinions from top surgeons for patients considering surgery.
  • Help patients understand treatment options and clear doubts transparently and supportively.
  • Connect patients with trusted Hyderabad-based surgeons for further evaluation or surgery, when needed.

Our Vision

To create a reliable, patient-friendly healthcare platform where clarity replaces confusion and trust replaces fear.

Our Motto

“Clear answers. Expert surgeons. Confident decisions.”

At AskSurgeons.com, we believe that every patient deserves the right opinion before making the big decision of surgery. Whether it’s a routine procedure or a complex transplant, our team is here to guide you every step of the way.
